Frequently Asked Questions

Understanding SOC, SIEM, and Sovereignty.

Where is your SOC located?
Outcomex operates a sovereign Australian SOC. All data analysis and personnel are located within Australia (Sydney & Melbourne), ensuring compliance with Privacy Act and data sovereignty requirements.
Do I need to buy a SIEM license?
We offer flexible models. We can manage your existing SIEM (Bring Your Own License) or provide a fully managed SIEM platform as part of the monthly service fee, saving you upfront capital expenditure.
What happens when you detect a threat?
Our SOAR automation immediately acts to contain the threat (e.g., isolating the host). Simultaneously, a Tier 2 analyst investigates. If it's a critical confirmed incident, we call you immediately (within 15 minutes) with a remediation plan or execute pre-approved response actions.
How much bandwidth does log collection use?
Log data is highly compressed before transmission. For most organizations, the impact on bandwidth is negligible. We deploy lightweight collectors on-premise that cache and throttle data to prevent network congestion.
