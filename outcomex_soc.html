The Evolution of Defense

Traditional SOCs focus on the 'boom'—the moment a hack happens. A Fusion Centre focuses on the 'left of boom'—preventing the conditions that allow attacks to succeed.

Traditional SOC

  • Reactive: Responds only after alerts trigger.
  • Siloed: Focuses only on logs and SIEM.
  • Technical Output: Confusing metrics for the Board.

Outcomex Fusion Centre

  • Proactive: Fixes vulnerabilities and missing controls before they are exploited.
  • Holistic: Fuses Compliance, Dark Web, and Vulnerability Data.
  • Business Context: Real-time risk reporting for Executives & Board.

Advanced Threat Detection

24/7 Eyes on Glass monitoring utilizing next-gen SIEM/SOAR. We separate the noise from the signal to detect complex, human-operated attacks.

Posture & Compliance
