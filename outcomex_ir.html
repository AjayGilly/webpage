What is IR Readiness & Planning?

Most organizations believe they are ready for a cyber attack because they have a backup server. Readiness goes far beyond technology.

It involves creating specific Playbooks for scenarios like Ransomware, Data Exfiltration, and Email Compromise. It defines Roles & Responsibilities (who calls the CEO? who shuts down the internet?), and it validates these plans through Tabletop Exercises (TTX).
