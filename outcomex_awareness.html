What is Security Awareness?

Cyber Security Awareness is the ongoing education of employees to recognize and report cyber threats. It moves beyond reading a policy document once a year to active engagement with real-world scenarios.

It encompasses Phishing Simulations, secure behavior training (password hygiene, physical security), and executive-level education, ensuring that every layer of the organization understands their role in protecting data.

Building the "Human Firewall"

The Training Cycle

  • Simulate Send realistic phishing emails to staff.
  • Educate Deliver "In-the-moment" micro-training if they click.
  • Measure Track "Click Rate" reduction over time.

Why It Matters to Business

You can spend millions on firewalls, but if one employee clicks a malicious link, the attacker is inside. People are the primary attack vector.

90% of Breaches

Research consistently shows that over 90% of successful cyber attacks start with a phishing email. Closing this gap provides the highest ROI in security.

Essential 8 Alignment

While "User Application Hardening" is technical, the ASD strongly advises user education as a compensating control for sophisticated social engineering attacks.

Cultural Shift

A mature security culture means employees report incidents fast without fear of punishment, allowing your SOC to contain threats quicker.
