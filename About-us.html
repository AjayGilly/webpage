Who We Are

Building the future since 2016.

Outcomex began in 2016 as an ambitious Australian systems integrator and has grown into one of the country's leading technology partners. We design, deliver and support complex technology environments across networking, cyber security, collaboration, cloud, data centre, managed services and intelligent edge solutions.

We invest in innovation and develop our own intellectual property to solve emerging business challenges. Our team combines deep technical knowledge with strategic thinking to bridge the gap between business objectives and IT infrastructure, delivering tailored solutions that create lasting value.

Our Leaders

Empowering innovation and excellence.

Strong leadership is about more than delivering results; it's about creating an environment where people, customers and innovation thrive.

Our leadership team combines decades of industry experience with a practical, customer-first approach. They lead by example, empowering our teams to think differently, solve complex challenges and continually raise the standard of service we deliver.

By fostering a culture built on collaboration, accountability and continuous improvement, our leaders ensure Outcomex remains agile, innovative and ready for what's next, for our customers, our partners and our people.

Marco Delgado

Chief Executive Officer

As Founder and Chief Executive Officer of Outcomex, Marco Delgado has led the company's growth from a specialist Australian systems integrator into a nationally recognised technology and innovation business. With a passion for engineering excellence and customer outcomes, Marco has shaped a culture built on technical expertise, long-term partnerships and continuous innovation. Under his leadership, Outcomex has expanded its national footprint, strengthened strategic vendor relationships, and developed award-winning technologies that continue to solve complex business challenges across multiple industries.

Michael van Zoggel

Managing Director

His focus on innovation, collaboration and technical excellence continues to strengthen Outcomex's position as a trusted technology partner.
