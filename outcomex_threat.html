What is Threat Intelligence & Hunting?

Threat Intelligence (TI) is the knowledge. It is knowing who is attacking (APTs, Crime Gangs), what tools they use, and why they are targeting your industry.

Threat Hunting is the action. It is the human-led process of proactively searching through your logs and endpoints for indicators of compromise that automated tools (EDR/SIEM) have missed. It assumes the breach has already happened.

Hypothesis-Driven Investigation

The Hunting Loop

  • Hypothesis "Is the 'Midnight Blizzard' actor in our network?"
  • Investigate Deep dive into logs, process trees, and memory.
  • Enrich Update SIEM rules to auto-detect next time.

Why Business Context Matters

Attacks are no longer random. They are targeted, patient, and human-operated. Automated defenses are not enough against a determined human adversary.

Find the "Silent" Attacker

Sophisticated attackers "live off the land," using legitimate admin tools (PowerShell, RDP) to evade detection. Only human hunters can spot this anomalous behavior.

Strategic Decisions

Knowing who targets you helps you budget. If you are targeted by Nation States vs. Script Kiddies, your defensive investment strategy must change.

Slash Dwell Time

The average time to detect a breach is over 200 days. Proactive hunting reduces this to hours, stopping data exfiltration before it impacts your reputation.
