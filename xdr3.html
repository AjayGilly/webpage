Why Managed XDR?

Managed XDR (MXDR) connects telemetry from your network, identity, and endpoints, pairing powerful correlation with a 24/7 team of sovereign experts to act on it.

1. See Everything

It breaks down silos. By ingesting logs from your entire security stack into one data lake, XDR provides a unified timeline of complex, multi-vector attacks that isolated tools miss.

2. Correlate with AI

The platform automatically groups related alerts into high-fidelity incidents. This slashes alert fatigue and immediately identifies the root cause of an intrusion.

3. Respond as a Service

Technology alone isn't enough. Our 24/7 Australian SOC actively hunts for threats and executes automated playbooks to contain breaches on your behalf at machine speed.
