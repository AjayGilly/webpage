What is the Essential 8?

Developed by the Australian Signals Directorate (ASD), the Essential 8 is widely considered the most effective baseline for cyber defence. It consists of eight mitigation strategies designed to prevent malware delivery and limit the extent of cyber security incidents.

Strategies are ranked by Maturity Levels (0 to 3). Moving from Level 0 (Unprepared) to Level 3 (Fully Aligned) drastically reduces your attack surface against sophisticated adversaries.

Defending Australia's Digital Borders

The Three Pillars

  • Prevent Attacks Application Control, Patch Applications, Configure Macros, User App Hardening
  • Limit Extent Restrict Admin Privileges, Patch OS, Multi-Factor Authentication
  • Recover Data Regular Backups

Why Alignment is Non-Negotiable

It is no longer just a "recommendation." The Essential 8 is becoming the standard of care for Australian business.

Stop Ransomware

The Essential 8 was specifically designed to neutralize the most common techniques used by ransomware gangs. Implementing "Application Control" alone stops 90% of executable-based malware.

Government Mandates

Under the SOCI Act and strict government procurement policies, aligning with Maturity Level 2 is often a prerequisite for doing business with the Commonwealth.

Insurance & Liability

Cyber insurance premiums are skyrocketing. Demonstrating E8 maturity lowers your premium and ensures your claim isn't denied due to "negligence."
